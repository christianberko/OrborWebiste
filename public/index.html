Who Are We?

Orobor, founded by tennis players at Boston University, is dedicated to revolutionizing recycling in the tennis and textile industries by transforming used tennis strings into high-purity polyester suitable for clothing manufacturing. Through innovation and collaboration, we aim to reduce waste, advance the circular economy, and inspire sustainable practices across industriesâ€”all while staying rooted in the values of teamwork and dedication we've learned on the court.

Our Process

Collect

We partner with tennis facilities and players to collect used polyester tennis strings, ensuring they are diverted from landfills and repurposed for recycling into high-quality materials.

Repurpose

Using advanced recycling techniques, we break down polyester tennis strings to remove impurities and additives, transforming them into high-purity polyester.

Collaborate

We aim to collaborate with clothing companies to integrate our high-quality recycled polyester into their supply chains, helping them create sustainable apparel.
