Who Are We?

Orobor, founded by tennis players at Boston University, is dedicated to revolutionizing recycling in the tennis and textile industries by transforming used tennis strings into high-purity polyester suitable for clothing manufacturing. Through innovation and collaboration, we aim to reduce waste, advance the circular economy, and inspire sustainable practices across industries—all while staying rooted in the values of teamwork and dedication we've learned on the court.
