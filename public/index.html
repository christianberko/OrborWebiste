One for the Court

At Orobor, we believe the game is bigger when everyone has the chance to play. That's why for every adult tee purchased, we donate a kids' tee to a young player who needs it most.

Through One for the Court, we work with nonprofit organizations around the world that use tennis to empower kids in underprivileged communities. Your purchase helps put apparel directly into the hands of the next generation, giving them the confidence and sense of belonging that comes with stepping onto the court.

Every shirt is more than apparel — it's an opportunity to grow the game and extend its reach to places where it can have the greatest impact. Together, we are giving back to the sport that has given us so much.

Who Are We?

Orobor, founded by tennis players at Boston University, is dedicated to revolutionizing recycling in the tennis and textile industries by transforming used tennis strings into high-purity polyester suitable for clothing manufacturing. Through innovation and collaboration, we aim to reduce waste, advance the circular economy, and inspire sustainable practices across industries—all while staying rooted in the values of teamwork and dedication we've learned on the court.
